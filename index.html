About Me

I am Ikhlas Hussain, and I am a realtor from Southern Massachusetts with years of experience in helping hundreds of clients find their dream home.


With a passion for excellent customer service and a deep understanding of the housing market, I'm committed to making your home-buying journey both as smooth and successful as I possibly can.


I currently work with one of the most experienced teams in Massachusetts real-estate, Camber Real Estate. We deal in residential, comercial buy and sell, new construction, auctions, rehabs, and rentals. We also provide all forms of insurance and mortgage services.


For the last 30 years, I worked at one of the most prestigious banks in the United States, U.S. Bancorp. As a banker, my areas of expertise included portfolio management, asset management, and relationship management. While working with some of the largest investment firms in the country, I managed a portfolio valued at $125 Billion.


I was also involved in a large-scale project at World Bank, where I prepared 100 feasibility reports involving the financing of various industrial projects in Pakistan.
